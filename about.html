We build the connective tissue for modern work

Stackly AI started with a simple frustration: the answers were always in the data, but never in the room when the decision got made.

Our story

From a spreadsheet nobody trusted to a platform 4,000 teams run on

In 2021 our founders were running operations at a logistics company. Every Monday they rebuilt the same forecast by hand, pulling numbers from six systems that disagreed with each other. By the time the report was ready, the decision window had closed.

So they built the thing they wanted: a workspace that reads from every system, reasons over it with AI, and hands people a recommendation they can act on — with the evidence attached.

  • Founded in 2021, profitable since 2024
  • 186 people across 14 countries, remote-first
  • Backed by Sequoia, Index and a lot of stubbornness

Our mission

Give every team the decision-making leverage that used to be reserved for companies with a data science department. Insight should arrive before the deadline, not after the post-mortem.

Our vision

A workplace where the busywork is handled by agents, the reasoning is transparent and auditable, and people spend their hours on the judgement calls only humans can make.

Core values

What we actually optimise for

Not posters on a wall — these are the tie-breakers we use when a decision is genuinely hard.

Trust is the product

An AI recommendation nobody believes is worse than no recommendation. Every output ships with its sources.

Ship small, ship often

We release roughly 40 times a week. Small changes are easier to reason about and easier to roll back.

Meet people where they are

Nobody wants another tab. Stackly lives inside Slack, your inbox and your CRM as much as in our own UI.
