Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your data — written to be read, not to be survived.

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect three categories of information:

  • Account data you give us — name, email, company, and billing details.
  • Workspace content you upload or connect — documents, records and the outputs of your workflows.
  • Usage data generated automatically — pages viewed, features used, IP address and device type.

2. How we use your information

To provide and secure the service, to bill you, to respond when you contact us, and to improve the product in aggregate. We do not sell your personal data, and we never have.

3. AI and model training

Your workspace content is never used to train shared or foundation models. Custom agents are fine-tuned within your isolated tenant and the resulting artefacts belong to you. You can delete them at any time from Settings → Security.

4. Data sharing

We share data only with sub-processors that are necessary to run the service (cloud hosting, payments, email delivery, error monitoring). The current list is published in our trust centre and we give 30 days’ notice before adding a new one.

5. Data retention

Workspace content is retained while your account is active and for 90 days after cancellation, so you can export it. Backups are purged within a further 30 days. Audit logs are kept for 12 months, or longer where law requires.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Email privacy@nebula.ai and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We hold SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications, run continuous vulnerability scanning, and commission an independent penetration test twice a year.

8. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and a single first-party analytics cookie to count page views. We do not run third-party advertising trackers.

9. Children

Nebula AI is a business product and is not directed at anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make a material change we will email account owners at least 30 days before it takes effect. The version history is kept in our trust centre.

Questions about this policy?

Write to legal@nebula.ai or post to Nebula AI, Inc., 488 Market Street, Floor 12, San Francisco, CA 94105.
