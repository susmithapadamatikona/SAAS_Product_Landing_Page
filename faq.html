Security & privacy

Never. Your workspace data is not used to train shared or foundation models. Custom agents are fine-tuned in an isolated tenant, and you can delete the artefacts at any time from Settings → Security.

SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR and HIPAA with a signed BAA. Reports are available under NDA, and penetration test summaries are published every six months.

By default in the region you choose at signup (US, EU or APAC). Enterprise plans can pin data residency to a specific region and supply their own encryption keys.

Billing

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades and cancellations apply at the end of the current billing period, and you keep full export access to your data for 90 days afterwards.

Your workspace switches to read-only until you pick a plan. Nothing is deleted for 90 days, and everything resumes exactly where you left off when you subscribe.

Registered non-profits get 50% off any plan. Accredited academic researchers get a free Professional workspace — just contact us from your institutional email.
